Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Aviel Gendler ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit avielg.com or contact me about my services.

Information I collect

I collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Your name and email address when you contact me via email or LinkedIn
  • Your phone number if you reach out via WhatsApp
  • Business details you share when discussing a project

I also collect basic usage data automatically through Vercel Analytics, including pages visited, time spent, browser type, and approximate geographic region. This data is aggregated and not linked to individual identities.

How I use your information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and discuss potential projects
  • Deliver agreed services and communicate about ongoing work
  • Send relevant updates if you have opted in

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and analytics

This site uses Vercel Analytics for page view tracking. Vercel Analytics is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or store personally identifiable data. No third-party advertising trackers are present on this site.

Data retention

Email correspondence and project-related communications are retained for as long as the professional relationship is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting me directly.

Third-party services

This site is hosted on Vercel. Contact forms link to LinkedIn and WhatsApp. Each of these services has its own privacy policy that governs data processed through their platforms.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR if you are in the EU/UK, or CCPA if you are in California) to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict how I process your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at aviel@gendler.com.

Changes to this policy

I may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of this site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email aviel@gendler.com.
